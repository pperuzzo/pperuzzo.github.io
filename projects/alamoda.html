overview

alamoda works with the best fashion boutiques in italy to provide designer fashion brands to the us market at highly discounted prices.
after personally establishing a partnership with one of the biggest boutiques in italy, i had to find a way to get high-quality products images starting from a list of skus.

i developed a custom web scraper from scratch using python and beautifulsoup to efficiently search and download product images starting from the product sku provided by the boutiques.
i designed the scraper to target specific keywords and gather relevant images, ensuring only high-quality and appropriate images.

i then created another python script to automatically add the scraped images on a predefined background and then apply relevant text as an overlay.
this allows for seamlessly generation of products picutres for alamoda's social media, saving time and allowing the marketing team to focus on other important tasks.

lastly, i developed a visually appealing landing page for the alamoda using plain html and css.
the landing pages provides visitors with essential information about the business, redirecting them to social media, and ultimately converting them into our customers.
