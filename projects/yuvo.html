overview

yuvo is a cross-platform ios and android app designed to help users discover nearby activities.
i developed the app from scratch using dart with flutter, to create native-like experiences for both platforms with a single codebase.

to provide users with an intuitive and engaging interface, i integrated the google maps api to allow users to easily discover nearby activities.

the simplicity of yuvo, combined with its powerful map-based functionality, made it a user-friendly and valuable tool for users looking to find new local activities.
